◆Introduction

 

Once upon a time, there's a pitiful girl.

She had a happy family.

She loves her father and mother so much.

However, her parents don't love each other.

 

She is treated aloof, and growing up without affection.

Having lost of parents, she decides to end her life.

The accidental visitor, the protagonist,

brings a glimmer of hope. Stopping what she will do

is the only issue you should think over!
