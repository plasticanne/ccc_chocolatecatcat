◆Introduction

 

The story takes place at the start of a university.

That day, just four students absent.

They with their own reasons, coincidentally at this point in time, to investigate an urban legend.

 

Among the four students, a girl wearing a cat ears lost consciousness during the investigation.

When she opened her eyes again, the sight of the ...... is completely unimaginable scene.

 

Wearing a cat ears of the girls will have to face many bizarre fairy tale.

The game, he must be one by one to solve these problems arising from the fairy tale, was able to move forward.

 

In the play also please you really, seriously took over the challenges in the game.

Because that, not just to give the role of the test.

But also to you the puzzle.
